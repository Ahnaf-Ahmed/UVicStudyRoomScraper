  Instructions:
  • Select the day you want to book a room for from the list on the right. You may book a room up to 7 days in advance.
  • Rooms are organized into five groups. Click on a group name above to see the available rooms.
  • Once you've found a room available at the time you want, click on the  symbol to book the room at the specified time. You will have to enter a valid Netlink ID and password to book the room. Each Netlink ID is limited to two hours of bookings per day.
  • You can delete a booking that you will be unable to use by clicking on the booking name and providing the same Netlink ID and password that were used to create it.
  • Library hours may be shortened on holidays, but the booking grid may not always reflect these changes. Please check the Library Hours page to verify that the library will be open at the time you want to use the room.
  • Study rooms must be vacated 15 minutes before the library closes.
  • For study room locations, check the Library Floor Maps or The Diana M. Priestly Law Library floor maps.
  • For study room booking guidelines, click here.
  • For more information about study rooms, check the library FAQs.
